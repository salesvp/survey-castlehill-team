01 · WHAT MEMBERS TOLD US

57 members responded over 1.5 weeks

The survey went out to current members, class pack holders, and people who'd previously tried Castle Hill. 81% of responses came from active members and pack holders. The rest came from people who'd tried us before but weren't currently signed up.

This summary covers what's working, what to focus on, what members want to see, and the conversion opportunities sitting in front of us.

03 · WHERE WE NEED TO FOCUS

The members have spoken.
Here's what came back consistently.

The next five slides cover the issues members raised most often. Each one shows the data, what members said, and is for us to discuss together. Action plans and rollout timelines will be worked out in the next management session.

04 · ISSUE 01

Cleanliness

MOST FREQUENTLY RAISED

Studio floor, equipment, and bathrooms flagged repeatedly

Cleanliness was raised independently by 12+ members across multiple questions. The specific complaints are consistent: hair on the floor under reformers, dust balls, equipment not wiped down, bathrooms needing more frequent cleaning, and toilet paper running out.

12+ members flagged cleanliness as needing improvement
WHAT MEMBERS SAID
Cleanliness is not great — often clumps of hair on machines or floor
I often find hair tumble-weeds beneath the carriage, and sometimes they are there for a few weeks
Bathrooms need to be cleaned better. Toilet paper needs more frequent re-stocking. Studio smells awful sometimes
I hate seeing hair everywhere under the beds — gives me major ick
The equipment is dirty, there are shoe marks. Routine cleaning of the equipment and studio at least weekly. I saw an insect last time
Given the majority female clients, more frequent vacuuming is needed to collect the hair balls that fly around
I often see large dust balls, and hair balls. More regular cleaning of floor please
05 · ISSUE 02

Stretching at the end of class

REPEATED REQUEST ACROSS MULTIPLE QUESTIONS

Members want a guided stretch component built into existing classes

Stretching came up repeatedly in the "what's missing" responses. Members aren't necessarily asking for a dedicated stretch class (though 67% would be interested in one — see next sections). They're asking for a few minutes of stretching at the end of regular classes.

5+ direct requests for stretching at end of class
WHAT MEMBERS SAID
I would like the sessions to finish with some good stretches
Stretching at the end of each session
Maybe 5 min at end of classes to be guided through a stretch / relaxed chat before leaving
Could we add a few minutes stretch before and after classes please
Post class stretch down
Stretching / mobility would be great
06 · ISSUE 03

Front of house presence at early classes

CONNECTION SCORE GAP

Members at early sessions feel they don't see front of house

Connection to the FOH team rated 3.29 out of 5 — significantly lower than connection to trainers (3.75). The open-text responses point to the same cause: early-class members rarely see anyone on the front desk. For many, the trainer is the only point of contact.

FOH connection: 3.29 vs Trainer connection: 3.75
WHAT MEMBERS SAID
I don't usually see them for the early sessions
Very limited interaction. Only recently realised there's a front of house team
I went to early classes, never saw front of house staff
It's always the trainer or no one there when I come in for class
Usually the front house is the trainer as well — probably because I train early slot
07 · ISSUE 04

Sauna

EQUIPMENT AND ACCESS ISSUES

Heating cycle, shower drainage, and access timing all flagged

Multiple members raised sauna issues. The most specific complaint is around the heating cycle — the sauna heats, turns off, then heats up again rather than maintaining constant temperature. Members also asked for sauna access around earlier and later class times.

6 members raised sauna issues
WHAT MEMBERS SAID
The sauna should be kept on or pre-heated before sessions rather than turned off after each session as you spend half the session waiting for the sauna to reach temperature. A sauna that maintains constant temperature would be better
I would love a sauna option to be open before or after the 9:30 classes pleaseee
Shower drainage — I use the shower often before metro to work and the sauna room shower is very slow to drain
The shower in the sauna tends to overflow and so showers need to be super quick
08 · ISSUE 05

Member connection to community

LOWEST RATED ITEM IN THE SURVEY

Community connection rated 3.04 out of 5

This is the weakest signal in the entire survey. Members rate everything else well — trainers, equipment, vibe, music — but the sense of belonging to a community at Castle Hill is sitting at 3.04. The open-text responses suggest the format makes connection hard: members come in, train, and leave with little chance to meet others.

Community: 3.04 · FOH connection: 3.29
WHAT MEMBERS SAID
There's not much time or opportunity to engage with others in the class
It's a very individual activity so find it hard to connect
More community events
I think maybe a little more energy from front of house. Everyone is lovely but I think it could perhaps be a bit more motivating when arriving
Maybe 5 min at end of classes to be guided through a stretch / relaxed chat before leaving
If I come more often I probably will feel more connected

10 · PATH 2 OPPORTUNITY

10 members tried us and didn't sign up. 9 of them would consider coming back.

What's sitting in front of us right now

Pricing was the #1 reason members didn't continue (70%). Life commitments came second (40%). The good news — almost every one of these members said they'd consider coming back if the issues were addressed, and 7 of 10 said yes to setting up a retry Intro Offer.

members tried but didn't sign up

One Path 2 respondent specifically asked for "a 20–30 pack valid for an extended time" for once-or-twice-a-week training. Worth raising when we discuss flexible pricing options.
